About us

Who and what is behind?



Hello! I'm Fernanda Oliveira. Nocturne World was created as my final project for CS50, an introductory Computer Science course. The aim was to bring information and knowledge from a subculture that suffers from prejudice and is unknown to many. And at the same time improve my programming skills. It was challenging, mainly because I had difficulties with the frontend when I first got in touch, but fortunately I managed to develop better until now. Thank you for reading and being interested.
