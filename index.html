What is Goth?

Welcome stranger and babybats!


Goth as a word, came from the “godo”, which were referred to a member of a Germanic people, and now, referred to the subculture. It started to be used in the meaning we have nowadays after the outcome of the Goth music. That’s why it’s a music-based subculture, but it’s also related to the gothic arts. This subculture, different from culture(cause here you can’t choose, you’re influenced), is about sharing enjoyment, excitement about the same subjects, interests, ideas(that also reflect their thoughts in politics). That also reflects in the aesthetic, but Goth is not about the outside, it’s about this sharing we’ve just talked about.
